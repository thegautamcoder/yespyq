Photo3.5 × 4.5 cm (275 × 354 px)20–100 KBJPGColour photo, white/light background, spectacles OK if eyes are visible

Frequently asked questions

What is the MPPSC photo size?
3.5×4.5 cm (275×354 pixels), 20–100 KB, in JPG, on a white or light background. Regular spectacles are fine as long as your eyes are clearly visible — dark glasses are not.
What is the MPPSC signature size?
3.5×1.5 cm (275×118 pixels), 10–100 KB, in JPG. Use black or blue ink on white paper and keep it sharp — blurry signatures photographed from a distance are rejected.
Does this apply to MPPSC Pre, Mains and ADPO?
The same photo and signature format is generally used across MP Public Service Commission exams, but the KB range can shift between notifications — confirm against the current one before applying.
Why does MPPSC reject photo uploads?
The usual causes are a file outside the 20–100 KB band, wrong pixel dimensions, a coloured or busy background, or dark glasses covering the eyes.
