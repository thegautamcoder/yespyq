Frequently asked questions

What is the RRB NTPC photo size?
Typically a colour passport-style photo at 200×230 pixels and 15–40 KB in JPG. The exact figures can shift a little between RRB regions, so read your zone's notification.
What is the RRB NTPC signature size?
Aim for roughly 200×80 pixels and 10–20 KB in JPG for the signature. Write it on white paper in blue or black ink.
Does each RRB zone have different photo requirements?
The basics stay the same everywhere — JPG, passport-style, white background — but the KB caps and precise pixels can differ slightly from one zone to another, so check the notification for your region.
Can I use a scanned photo for RRB NTPC?
You can. Scan a passport-size print at 200 DPI or higher, then bring it down to the required dimensions and KB range using our tool.
What happens if I upload a wrong photo size for RRB NTPC?
The portal turns away any file that breaks the size or dimension limits. Resize it with our tool and submit it again.
Is the RRB NTPC photo requirement different from RRB Group D?
They share the same broad format — a JPG colour photo on a white background — but the exact specs can vary, so verify the pixel and KB rules separately for each exam.
