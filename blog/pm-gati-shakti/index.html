PM Gati Shakti Explained

By YESPYQ · Updated July 2026 · 5 min read

PM Gati Shakti is a national master plan for multimodal connectivity that aims to integrate the infrastructure planning of various ministries and departments on a single digital platform.

Integrated planning

It brings together railways, roads, ports, airports and other infrastructure on a common GIS-based platform, so projects are planned and executed in a coordinated, holistic manner.

Goal

The plan seeks to reduce logistics costs, remove bottlenecks and improve the efficiency of moving goods and people, supporting the National Logistics Policy.

Why it matters for UPSC

PM Gati Shakti's multimodal, integrated-planning approach is a commonly tested infrastructure scheme.
