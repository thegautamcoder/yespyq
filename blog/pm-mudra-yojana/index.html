Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY) Explained

By YESPYQ · Updated July 2026 · 5 min read

The Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY) provides collateral-free institutional credit to micro and small non-corporate, non-farm enterprises.

Loan categories

Loans are given in three categories based on the stage of the business: Shishu (smallest loans), Kishor (mid-range) and Tarun (largest). They are routed through banks and financial institutions.

Purpose

The scheme supports small entrepreneurs, income-generating activities and self-employment, promoting financial inclusion.

Why it matters for UPSC

The Mudra loan categories (Shishu, Kishor, Tarun) and the collateral-free feature are commonly tested scheme facts.

Frequently asked questions

What are the three categories of Mudra loans?

Shishu, Kishor and Tarun, based on loan size and business stage.

Are Mudra loans collateral-free?

Yes. PMMY provides collateral-free credit to eligible micro and small enterprises.
