The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) is the government's flagship housing scheme, aiming to provide affordable housing to eligible families.

Two components

PMAY has two arms: PMAY-Gramin (rural) for building pucca houses in villages, and PMAY-Urban for urban housing, including credit-linked subsidies and affordable housing in partnership.

Objective

The scheme targets 'housing for all', focusing on the poor and economically weaker sections, with features like basic amenities and beneficiary-led construction.

Why it matters for UPSC

PMAY's two components (Gramin and Urban) and the 'housing for all' goal are commonly tested scheme facts.
