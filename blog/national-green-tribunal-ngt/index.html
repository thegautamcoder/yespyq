The National Green Tribunal (NGT) is a specialised statutory body established under the NGT Act, 2010, for the effective and expeditious disposal of environmental cases.

Jurisdiction

The NGT handles cases relating to environmental protection, forests and other natural resources, and can order relief, compensation and restoration of the environment.

Significance

It applies principles like sustainable development, the polluter-pays principle and the precautionary principle, making environmental justice more accessible.

Why it matters for UPSC

The NGT's statutory basis (2010 Act) and the principles it applies are commonly tested Environment and Polity facts.
