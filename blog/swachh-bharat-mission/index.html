Swachh Bharat Mission Explained

By YESPYQ · Updated July 2026 · 5 min read

The Swachh Bharat Mission is a nationwide campaign to improve sanitation and cleanliness, with rural and urban components.

Goals

Its first phase focused on eliminating open defecation by building toilets and changing behaviour, leading to many areas being declared Open Defecation Free (ODF). Later phases focus on solid and liquid waste management and sustaining ODF status.

Significance

The mission links sanitation to health, dignity and environmental outcomes, and is one of the largest such campaigns globally.

Why it matters for UPSC

The Swachh Bharat Mission's ODF goal and its focus on waste management in later phases are commonly tested topics.
