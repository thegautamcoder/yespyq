Startup India Initiative Explained

By YESPYQ · Updated July 2026 · 5 min read

Startup India is a flagship initiative to build a strong ecosystem for nurturing innovation and startups in the country.

Support offered

It offers benefits such as self-certification, tax exemptions for eligible startups, easier compliance, and access to funding through a Fund of Funds for Startups.

Recognition

Startups recognised by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) can avail these benefits, helping India become one of the world's largest startup ecosystems.

Why it matters for UPSC

Startup India's benefits and DPIIT recognition are commonly tested economy and governance facts.
