Asian Development Bank (ADB) Explained

By YESPYQ · Updated July 2026 · 5 min read

The Asian Development Bank (ADB) is a regional development bank established in 1966 to promote social and economic development in Asia and the Pacific. It is headquartered in Manila.

Role

ADB provides loans, technical assistance and grants for infrastructure, education, health and environmental projects, and works to reduce poverty in the region.

India and ADB

India is a founding member and a major borrower, using ADB financing for infrastructure and development projects.

Why it matters for UPSC

ADB's headquarters (Manila), founding year and development role are commonly tested facts.
