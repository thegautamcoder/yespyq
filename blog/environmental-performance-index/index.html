Environmental Performance Index (EPI) Explained

By YESPYQ · Updated July 2026 · 4 min read

The Environmental Performance Index (EPI) ranks countries on environmental health and ecosystem vitality. It is produced by researchers at Yale and Columbia universities.

What it measures

The EPI uses indicators across categories such as climate change mitigation, environmental health (air quality, sanitation) and ecosystem vitality (biodiversity, forests).

Debate

India has questioned certain indicators and weightings, arguing they do not fully reflect developing-country contexts.

Why it matters for UPSC

The EPI's producers and its environmental indicators feature in Environment current-affairs questions.
