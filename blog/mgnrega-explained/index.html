MGNREGA (Rural Employment Guarantee)

By YESPYQ · Updated July 2026 · 6 min read

The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA), 2005, provides a legal guarantee of wage employment to rural households, making the right to work justiciable.

The guarantee

MGNREGA guarantees at least 100 days of unskilled manual wage employment in a financial year to every rural household whose adult members volunteer to do such work.

Demand-driven and rights-based

Unlike earlier schemes, it is demand-driven — work must be provided within 15 days of a request, failing which an unemployment allowance is payable. This makes it a rights-based programme.

Transparency and assets

It mandates social audits by gram sabhas and focuses on creating durable rural assets like water conservation structures and rural roads.

Why it matters for UPSC

MGNREGA is a perennial favourite — its legal 100-day guarantee, demand-driven nature, unemployment allowance and social audit mechanism are all commonly tested.

Frequently asked questions

How many days of employment does MGNREGA guarantee?

At least 100 days of unskilled manual wage employment per rural household per financial year.

What happens if work is not provided under MGNREGA?

If work is not provided within 15 days of demand, the applicant is entitled to an unemployment allowance.
