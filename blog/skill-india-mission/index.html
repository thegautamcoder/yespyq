The Skill India Mission is a national effort to train a large number of people in industry-relevant skills to improve employability and productivity.

Key scheme

A flagship component is the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), which provides short-term skill training and certification, including recognition of prior learning.

Ecosystem

The mission works through the National Skill Development Corporation, sector skill councils and training institutes to align skilling with industry needs.

Why it matters for UPSC

Skill India and PMKVY are commonly tested in the context of human capital and employment.
