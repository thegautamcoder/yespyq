PM-KISAN Scheme

By YESPYQ · Updated July 2026 · 6 min read

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) is a central-sector scheme launched to provide direct income support to landholding farmer families across India.

What the scheme provides

Eligible farmer families receive ₹6,000 per year, paid in three equal instalments of ₹2,000, transferred directly into bank accounts via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).

Who is eligible

It covers landholding farmer families, subject to exclusion criteria (such as income-tax payers, institutional landholders and certain government employees). It is a 100% centrally funded scheme.

Significance

By using Aadhaar-linked DBT, PM-KISAN minimises leakages and provides predictable supplementary income to help farmers meet input costs.

Why it matters for UPSC

Government schemes like PM-KISAN are frequent Prelims targets — the ministry, the exact benefit amount and the funding pattern (central-sector vs centrally-sponsored) are commonly tested.

Frequently asked questions

How much does PM-KISAN provide?

₹6,000 per year in three instalments of ₹2,000 each, directly to farmer families' bank accounts.

Is PM-KISAN a central-sector scheme?

Yes, it is a 100% central-sector scheme fully funded by the Government of India.
