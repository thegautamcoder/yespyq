About YESPYQ

Updated July 2026

YESPYQ is a free, no-login web app for practising UPSC Civil Services Examination (IAS) Prelims previous year questions (PYQs). We believe the smartest way to crack the Prelims is to master the questions UPSC has already asked — so we took thousands of authentic past questions, cleaned them up, organised them subject-wise and year-wise, and paired every one with a clear answer and explanation. All of it, free.

YESPYQ by the numbers

  • 2,100+ authentic PYQs from UPSC CSE Prelims (General Studies Paper-I).
  • 7 subjects — Polity & Governance, History & Art-Culture, Geography, Economy, Environment & Ecology, Science & Technology, and Current Affairs.
  • ~3 decades of past papers, browsable year by year.
  • Every question solved — correct answer plus a detailed explanation.

What makes YESPYQ different

  • Only real PYQs. Every question actually appeared in a UPSC Prelims paper and is tagged with its subject and exam year — no mock filler.
  • One question at a time. A focused, gamified quiz mode with XP, combos and daily streaks keeps practice engaging without overwhelming you.
  • Answers that teach. Explanations focus on the concept being tested, not just the right option.
  • Free, fast, everywhere. No login, works offline as a mobile app (PWA), with light and dark modes.

How it works

  • 1. Pick your mode — take a quick 10-question quiz, or browse by subject or exam year.
  • 2. Solve & learn — answer, see instant feedback, and read the explanation.
  • 3. Build the habit — earn XP and keep your streak going, day after day.

Why previous year questions?

PYQs are the most reliable guide to how UPSC actually thinks — the depth it tests, the way it frames options, and the themes it returns to year after year. Practising them is the single highest-return activity in Prelims preparation. Learn the method in our PYQ strategy guide, or read how to become an IAS officer.

Our commitment

YESPYQ is and will remain free to use, with no login required. We are an independent study tool and are not affiliated with or endorsed by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC). UPSC and the Civil Services Examination are conducted solely by the Commission.
