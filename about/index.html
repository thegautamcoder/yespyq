About YESPYQ

Last updated June 2026

YESPYQ is a free, no-login platform for practising UPSC Civil Services Examination (CSE) Prelims previous year questions (PYQs). Our goal is simple: help aspirants revise smarter by organising authentic Prelims-style questions subject-wise and year-wise, each with an answer key and a crisp explanation.

What we offer

  • Subject-wise practice across Polity, History, Geography, Economy, Environment, Science & Technology and Current Affairs.
  • Year-wise practice so you can attempt questions from a specific exam year.
  • Instant answers and explanations that focus on the concept being tested, not just the correct option.
  • A growing preparation blog with PYQ analysis, weightage trends and strategy — see the YESPYQ blog.

Why PYQs?

Previous year questions are the most reliable guide to how UPSC actually thinks — the depth it tests, the way it frames options, and the themes it returns to. We built YESPYQ so that this resource is free, fast and well-organised for every aspirant.

Our commitment

YESPYQ is and will remain free to use, with no login required. We are an independent study tool and are not affiliated with or endorsed by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC).
