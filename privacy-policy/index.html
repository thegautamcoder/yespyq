Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how YESPYQ ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use yespyq.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

YESPYQ is a free study tool that does not require you to create an account or log in. We do not ask for your name, email or any personal identifiers to use the question bank.

  • Usage data: Like most websites, we collect anonymous, aggregated analytics (pages visited, device type, approximate region, time on page) to understand how the Site is used and improve it.
  • Local storage: Your browser may store progress, streaks or preferences locally on your device. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Information you send us: If you email us (for example, to report a question error), we receive the contents of that message and your email address.

2. Cookies & analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to measure traffic and usage trends. Google Analytics may set cookies and process data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. This data is used in aggregate and does not personally identify you. You can disable cookies in your browser settings or use the Google Analytics Opt-out add-on.

3. Third-party services

The Site is hosted on third-party infrastructure and may serve assets via content delivery networks. These providers may process limited technical data (such as IP address) as part of delivering the Site securely.

4. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain and improve the Site and its content.
  • To understand which subjects and features are most useful to aspirants.
  • To respond to messages you send us.

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Children's privacy

YESPYQ is intended for exam aspirants, generally adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your choices

You can browse YESPYQ without providing personal information, clear local storage from your browser at any time, and opt out of analytics cookies as described above.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, email teamyespyq@gmail.com or visit our contact page.
