Age is fixed as on the first day of the course commencement month. On qualification, you must have passed Class 12 (10+2) or be appearing for it — any stream works for the Army, but the Navy and Air Force require Physics and Mathematics at Class 12.

How NDA age eligibility works

The NDA & NA age bar is enforced to the letter, and — unlike most UPSC exams — it carries no category relaxation whatsoever. Fall outside the window by a single day and you are ruled out for that cycle.

The date that counts is the first day of the course commencement month, not the day you fill the form or write the exam. Your age on that fixed cut-off is what settles eligibility.

Note: the age bars and rules can shift with each notification. Use this calculator for a quick check, then confirm your eligibility against the official NDA notification.

Frequently asked questions

What is the NDA age limit?
You have to be 16.5 to 19.5 years old on the first day of the month the course starts. NDA grants no age relaxation to any category — SC, ST or OBC.
Is there age relaxation for OBC/SC/ST?
No — NDA breaks from the other UPSC exams here and extends no relaxation to reserved categories. The same 16.5 to 19.5 year window applies to everyone.
Can girls apply for NDA?
Yes. From 2022 onwards, women can also sit the NDA examination under the Army wing.
How many times is NDA held each year?
Twice — UPSC runs NDA I (notified in January, exam in April) and NDA II (notified around May/June, exam in September). You can take both, provided you remain inside the age window.
What subjects are required for NDA?
The Army wing accepts Class 12 in any stream, but the Air Force and Navy insist on Physics and Mathematics at Class 12. Without those two subjects, the Army wing is your only option.
What is the NDA photo size requirement?
A colour photo of 400x400 pixels (or a similar square format), 20 to 100 KB, in JPG. Confirm the exact specifications in the current notification.
What if I exceed the 19.5-year upper limit?
Cross 19.5 and you are permanently out of NDA, since the cap allows no relaxation. That said, CDS opens up to you once you graduate.
