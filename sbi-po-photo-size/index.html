Frequently asked questions

What is the SBI PO photo size?
Use a recent colour photograph — clicked within three months — measuring 200×230 pixels at 20–50 KB in JPG, taken against a plain white background.
What is the SBI PO signature size?
The signature is uploaded at 140×60 pixels and 10–20 KB in JPG. Sign on plain white paper using black or dark blue ink.
Is SBI PO photo size different from IBPS PO?
The two line up closely — 200×230 px, 20–50 KB, JPG for both. Even so, check the latest SBI notification, because banks do occasionally tweak the numbers.
Does SBI PO require a thumb impression upload?
In certain cycles SBI also asks for a left thumb impression along with the photo and signature. Look up this year's notification to be sure.
What if my SBI PO photo upload fails?
Run the image through our resizer to land it at 200×230 px, under 50 KB, in JPG, then attempt the upload again.
Can I use the same photo for SBI PO and SBI Clerk?
You can, provided it satisfies both — and SBI exams generally share the same rules. That said, a freshly taken photo always looks best.
