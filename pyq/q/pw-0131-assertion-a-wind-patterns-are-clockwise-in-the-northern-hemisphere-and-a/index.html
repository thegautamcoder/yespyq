Assertion (A) : Wind patterns are clockwise in the Northern Hemisphere and anti-clockwise in the Southern Hemisphere. Reason (R) : The directions of wind patterns in the North- ern and the Southern Hemisphere are governed by the Co- riolis effect.

aBoth A and R are true and R is the correct explanation of A
bBoth A and R are true but R is NOT a correct explanation of A
cA is true but R is false
dA is false but R is true
✓ Correct answer: d) A is false but R is true
ExplanationAssertion (A) is false: Wind patterns in the Northern Hemisphere move clockwise around high-pressure systems and counterclockwise around low-pressure systems.In the Southern Hemisphere, the pattern is opposite: counter- clockwise around high-pressure systems and clockwise around low-pressure systems.The statement in the Assertion (A) is incorrect because it gener- alizes wind patterns without distinguishing between high-pres- sure and low-pressure systems.Reason (R) is true: The Coriolis effect, caused by the Earth's ro- tation, deflects winds to the right in the Northern Hemisphere and to the left in the Southern Hemisphere.This influences the direction of wind patterns, making R correct.Conclusion: Since A is false but R is true, the correct answer is (d): 52
