a1 and 2 only
b3 only
c1 and 3 only
d1, 2 and 3
✓ Correct answer: b) 3 only
ExplanationBabur successfully conquered Bhira (1519–1520), Sialkot (1520), and Lahore (1524) in Punjab.The decisive encounter between Babur and Ibrahim Lodi took place at the Battle of Panipat in 1526. Despite having fewer soldiers, Babur’s army was better organized and strategically superior.Babur’s battlefield tactics were distinctive, he strategically flanked Ibrahim’s army on both sides while his cavalry launched a central assault using arrows and gunfire from skilled Ottoman gunners.Statement 1 is incorrect: Gunpowder was first used in the Indian subcontinent during the 14th century earlier than Babur’s arrival .However it gained prominence during Babur’s campaign for control of Bhera in India in 1519.
