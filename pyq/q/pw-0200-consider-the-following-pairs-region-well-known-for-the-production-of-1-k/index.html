Consider the following pairs : Region Well-known for the production of1. Kinnaur Areca nut2. Mewat Mango3. Coromandel Soya beanWhich of the above pairs is/are correctly matched?

a1 and 2 only
b3 only
c1, 2 and 3
dNone
✓ Correct answer: d) None
ExplanationPair 1 is incorrectly matched : Kinnaur is primarily known for its apple orchards, not for Areca nut production.The region’s agriculture is most famous for apples, along with other fruits like apricots and peaches.Areca nut is mostly produced in states like Karnataka, Kerala, and Assam, which have the ideal climatic conditions for this crop.Pair 2 is incorrectly matched : Mewat is not particularly famous for mango production.The region is known for agriculture and allied agro-based activities, but mango cultivation is not a pri- mary feature of Mewat.The major mango-growing states in In- dia include Andhra Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Karnataka, Bihar, and West Bengal.Pair 3 is incorrectly matched : Coromandel Coast is primarily known for the production of crops like rice, pulses, sugarcane, cotton, and peanuts.It is not known for soybean production.The major states producing soybeans in India are Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh, which together contribute 89% of the to- tal production of soybeans in the country.132
