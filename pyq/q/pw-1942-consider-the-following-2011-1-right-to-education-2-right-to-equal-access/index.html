Consider the following: [2011]1. Right to education2. Right to equal access to public service3. Right to foodWhich of the above is/are Human Right/Human Rights under "Universal Declaration of Human Rights" ?

a1 only
b1 and 2 only
c3 only
d1, 2 and 3
✓ Correct answer: d) 1, 2 and 3
ExplanationThe Universal Declaration of Human Rights (UDHR), adopted by the United Nations General Assembly in 1948, enshrines several fundamental human rights.Let’s analyze each of the given rights:Right to Education: Article 26 of UDHR explicitly recognizes the right to education.It states that education shall be free, at least in the elementary and fundamental stages, and that higher education shall be equally accessible to all based on merit.Right to Equal Access to Public Service: Article 21(2) of UDHR guarantees that everyone has the right to equal access to public service in their country.This ensures that no one is discriminated against in accessing government-provided services.Right to Food: Article 25(1) of UDHR recognizes the right to an adequate standard of living, including food, clothing, housing, and medical care.This establishes food as a fundamental human right necessary for survival and well- being.58
