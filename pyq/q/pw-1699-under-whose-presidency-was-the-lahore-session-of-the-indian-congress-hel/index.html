Under whose presidency was the Lahore session of the Indian Congress held in the year 1929, wherein a resolution was adopted to gain complete independence from the British?

aBal Gangadhar Tilak
bGopal Krishna Gokhale
cJawaharlal Nehru
dMotilal Nehru
✓ Correct answer: c) Jawaharlal Nehru
ExplanationThe Lahore session of the Indian National Congress was held in 1929 under the presidency of Jawaharlal Nehru.During this session, the Congress adopted the historic resolution to demand Purna Swaraj (complete independence) from British rule.It was also decided to celebrate 26th January 1930 as Independence Day, marking the declaration of India’s resolve for full sovereignty.This session marked a significant shift in the freedom struggle, moving away from dominion status demands to the goal of complete independence.84
