The Bermuda Triangle extends up to which of the following places?1. Southern Florida2. Puerto Rico3. Hawaiian IslandsSelect the correct answer using the codes given below :

a1, 2 and 3
b1 and 2 only
c2 and 3 only
d1 and 3 only Geography 455
✓ Correct answer: b) 1 and 2 only
ExplanationOptions 1 and 2 are correct : The Bermuda Triangle extends between Southern Florida, Puerto Rico, and Bermuda, form- ing a triangular region in the Atlantic Ocean.The Bermuda Triangle, also known as the Devil’s Triangle, is a loosely de- fined region where several ships and aircraft are said to have mysteriously disappeared.Option 3 is incorrect : Hawaiian Islands are located in the Pa- cific Ocean, far from the Bermuda Triangle, and are not part of it.453
