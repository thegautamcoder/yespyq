Pneumoconiosis affects the workers who work mainly in

aTanneries
bCoal mining industry
cDistillerie
dGlass industry
✓ Correct answer: b) Coal mining industry
ExplanationWorkers in the coal mining industry are most affected by pneumoconiosis.Pneumoconiosis is a lung disease caused by the inhalation of dust, particularly mineral or industrial dust over a prolonged period.It primarily affects workers in industries where they are exposed to high levels of airborn e particles.Coal workers’ pneumoconiosis (CWP), also known as black lung disease, is caused by prolonged inhalation of coal dust, making coal miners the most affected group.Other forms of pneumoconiosis include silicosis (caused by silica dust, affecting quarry and glass industry workers) and asbestosis (caused by asbestos exposure).443
