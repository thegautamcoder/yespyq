If you travel by road from Kohima to Kottayam, what is the minimum number of States within India through which you can travel, including the origin and the destination?

a6
b7
c8
d9
✓ Correct answer: b) 7
ExplanationOption (b) is correct : Traveling by road from Kohima in Nagaland to Kottayam in Kerala involves traversing multi- ple Indian states.To determine the minimum number of states one would pass through, including the origin and destination, let’s analyze the shortest route:Nagaland: Starting point at Kohima.Assam: Proceed westward into Assam.West Bengal: Continue through Assam into West Bengal.Odisha: Travel southward from West Bengal into Odisha.Andhra Pradesh: Proceed further south into Andhra Pradesh.Tamil Nadu: From Andhra Pradesh, enter Tamil Nadu.Kerala: Finally, arrive at Kottayam in Kerala.UPSC GS Paper-I PYQs 450 This route covers seven states in total.Alternatively, after Andhra Pradesh, one could enter Karnataka before reaching Ker- ala, still totaling seven states.431
