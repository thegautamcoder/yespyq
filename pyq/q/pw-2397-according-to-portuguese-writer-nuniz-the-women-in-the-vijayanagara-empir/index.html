According to Portuguese writer Nuniz, the women in the Vijayanagara Empire were experts in which of the following areas?1. Wrestling2. Astrology3. Accounting4. SoothsayingSelect the correct answer using the code given below.

a1, 2 and 3 only
b1, 3 and 4 only
c2 and 4 only
d1, 2, 3 and 4
✓ Correct answer: d) 1, 2, 3 and 4
ExplanationThe accounts of foreign travelers such as Abdur Razzak from Persia, Nicolo de Conte from Italy, Fernao Nuniz, Domingo Paes and Duarte Barbosa from Portugal, provide us with a valuable historical lens of the Vijayanagara Kingdom.Fernao Nuniz, a Portuguese traveler who visited the Vijayanagara Empire during the reign of Achyuta Deva Raya, noted that women in the empire held prominent roles.He observed that they were trained in various skills, including wrestling, swordsmanship, soothsaying, astrology, and accounting.These women were actively involved in the kingdom’s political, social, and cultural life.In addition to fine arts like music, some also received education in literature​.SUPER HINT You can try, The "NOT" approach for all statements, this tests the improbability of negating a statement—if de- nying its impact seems highly unlikely, the statement is plausibly true.Nuniz would’ve recorded exceptions, so probability of all being true is very high.44
