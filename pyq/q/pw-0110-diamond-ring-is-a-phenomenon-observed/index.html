“Diamond ring” is a phenomenon observed :

aat the start of a total solar eclipse
bat the end of a total solar eclipse
conly along the peripheral regions of the totality trail
donly in the central regions of the totality trail
✓ Correct answer: c) only along the peripheral regions of the totality trail
ExplanationThe “Diamond Ring” phenomenon is a striking opti- cal effect observed during a total solar eclipse.It occurs when a small sliver of the Sun’s light is visible around the edge of the Moon, creating the appearance of a brilliant diamond resting on a luminous ring.This effect is typically seen moments just before the eclipse reaches totality or immediately after it ends.2.
