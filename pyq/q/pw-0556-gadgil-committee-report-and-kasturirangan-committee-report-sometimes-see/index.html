‘Gadgil Committee Report’ and ‘Kasturirangan Committee Report’, sometimes seen in the news, are related to

aconstitutional reforms
bGanga Action Plan
clinking of rivers
dprotection of Western Ghats
✓ Correct answer: d) protection of Western Ghats
ExplanationThe Western Ghats Ecology Expert Panel (WGEEP), also known as Gadgil Committee was chaired by Prof. Madhav Gadgil.It was established by the Ministry of Environment & For- ests in 2010 and submitted its report in 2011. The panel was con- stituted to primarily demarcate ecologically sensitive areas in the Western Ghats and recommended measures for the management of these ecologically sensitive areas.The Kasturirangan com- mittee was constituted to examine the WGEEP report in 2012. The key recommendations were:Declaring the entire Western Ghats as an Ecologically Sensitive Area (ESA).Dividing the region into three zones (Ecologically Sensitive Zones 1, 2, and 3), with strict regulations on mining, industries, and land-use changes.Implementing bottom-up democratic decision-making by involving local communities in conservation efforts.Banning large-scale developmental projects in sensitive zones.35
