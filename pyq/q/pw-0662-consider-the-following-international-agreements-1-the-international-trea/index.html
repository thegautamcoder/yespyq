Consider the following international agreements:1. The International Treaty on Plant Genetic Resources for Food and Agriculture.2. The United Nations Convention to Combat Desertification.3. The World Heritage ConventionWhich of the above has/have a bearing on biodiversity?

a1 and 2 only
b3 only
c1 and 3 only
d1, 2 and 3
✓ Correct answer: d) 1, 2 and 3
ExplanationOption 1 is correct: The International Treaty on Plant Genet- ic Resources for Food and Agriculture, also known as the Seed Treaty, was adopted during the 31st session of the United Na- tions Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) in 2001. India is a signatory to this treaty, which directly impacts biodiversity, as seeds are crucial for the conservation of plant genetic resources.Option 2 is correct: United Nations Convention to Combat Desertification (UNCCD) was established in 1994.
