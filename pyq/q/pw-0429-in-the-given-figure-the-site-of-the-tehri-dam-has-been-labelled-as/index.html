In the given figure, the site of the Tehri dam has been labelled as:

aA
bB
cC
dD
✓ Correct answer: c) C
ExplanationThe Tehri Dam is located in the Tehri Garhwal dis- trict of Uttarakhand.It is the tallest dam in India and one of the highest in the world.It is built on the Bhagirathi River near its confluence with the Bhilangna River.The dam plays a crucial role in hydroelectric power generation, irrigation, and municipal wa- ter supply.Its construction began in 1978 and was completed in 2006 and is managed by THDC India Limited.The dam generates approximately 1,000 MW of renewable energy, significantly con- tributing to India’s power infrastructure.Tehri Lake has emerged as a popular destination for adventure tourism, offering various water sports and recreational activities.UPSC GS Paper-I PYQs 438 397
