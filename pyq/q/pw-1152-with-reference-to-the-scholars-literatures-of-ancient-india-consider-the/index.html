With reference to the scholars/literatures of ancient India, consider the following statements:1. Panini is associated with Pushyamitra Shunga.2. Amarasimha is associated with Harshavardhana.3. Kalidasa is associated with Chandra Gupta-II.Which of the statements given above is/are correct?

a1 and 2 only
b2 and 3 only
c3 only
d1, 2 and 3 UPSC GS Paper-I PYQs 50
✓ Correct answer: c) 3 only
