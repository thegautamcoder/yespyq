Cape Canaveral, the site from which space shuttles are launched is located on the coast of :

aFlorida
bVirginia
cNorth Carolina
dSouth Carolina
✓ Correct answer: a) Florida
ExplanationCape Canaveral, also called Cape Kennedy, is a major spaceport on Florida’s east coast.It serves as the primary launch site for U.S. space missions.It was established in 1949 as a mis- sile testing site, Cape Canaveral has played a crucial role in space exploration, evolving into a key launch site.Being positioned on a barrier island along Florida’s east coast, its coastal setting offers ideal conditions for launching spacecraft into orbit.While Virginia has NASA’s Wallops Flight Facility, it is not used for space shuttle launches.North Carolina and South Carolina also lack any major spaceport like Cape Canaveral.440
