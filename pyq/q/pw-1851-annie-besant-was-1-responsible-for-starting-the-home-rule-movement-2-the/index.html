Annie Besant was:1. Responsible for starting the Home Rule Movement2. The founder of the Theosophical Society3. Once the President of the Indian National Congress Select the correct statement/statements using the codes given below.

a1 only
b2 and 3 only
c1 and 3 only
d1, 2 and 3
✓ Correct answer: c) 1 and 3 only
ExplanationAnnie Besant (1847–1933) made her first visit to India as a member of the Theosophical Society.She had embraced Theosophy after meeting Helena Blavatsky in 1889. Following her arrival, the society relocated its headquarters to Adyar, near Madras (now Chennai).Besant went on to serve as the society’s president from 1907 until her passing in 1933.
