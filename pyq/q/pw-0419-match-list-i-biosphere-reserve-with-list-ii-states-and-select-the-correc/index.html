bA-4; B-5; C -2; D-1
cA-1; B-5; C-2; D-4
dA-4; B-3; C-5; D-1 UPSC GS Paper-I PYQs 434
✓ Correct answer: d) A-4; B-3; C-5; D-1 UPSC GS Paper-I PYQs 434
ExplanationA is correctly matched with 4 : Similipal Biosphere Reserve is located in the Mayurbhanj district of Odisha.Similipal Bio- sphere Reserve is renowned for its diverse ecosystems, encom- passing dense forests, grasslands, and wetlands.The reserve is a significant habitat for the Bengal tiger and the Asian elephant.The Similipal biosphere reserve is an important part of the Ma- yurbhanj Elephant Reserve which also includes the Kuldiha Wildlife Sanctuary and Hadgarh Wildlife Sanctuary.B is correctly matched with 3 : Dehang-Debang Biosphere Reserve is situated in the eastern Himalayan region of Arunachal Pradesh.The Brahmaputra River, known as the Siang and Dihang in Arunachal Pradesh, flows through the reserve.Dehang-Debang is a crucial habitat for several endan- gered species such as Mishmi Takin and the Red Panda.C is correctly matched with 5 : Nokrek Biosphere Reserve is in the West Garo Hills of Meghalaya.It is home to the rare and endangered species of the Red Panda.The reserve is also famous for being the origin of Meghalaya’s important rivers including the Simsang River.
