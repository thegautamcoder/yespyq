Which one of the following types of coal contains a higher percentage of carbon than the rest ?

aBituminous coal
bLignite
cPeat
dAnthracite
✓ Correct answer: d) Anthracite
ExplanationAnthracite is a hard, brittle coal characterized by its high carbon content, typically ranging from 86% to 98%.It is considered the most metamorphosed type of coal, meaning it has undergone the most intense heat and pressure during its formation.This high carbon content makes anthracite the clean- est-burning type of coal, producing very little smoke or volatile matter.It is often referred to as “hard coal” and burns with a short, blue flame.Due to its cleaner-burning properties, an- thracite is a preferred fuel source where minimizing air pol- lution is a priority.However, its relative scarcity and higher cost compared to other coal types limit its widespread use.508
