Consider the following:1. Mahadeo Hills2. Sahyadri Parvat3. Satpura Range What is the correct sequence of the above from the north to the south ?

a1, 2, 3
b2, 1, 3
c1, 3, 2
d2, 3, 1
✓ Correct answer: c) 1, 3, 2
ExplanationMahadev Hills is located in Madhya Pradesh.They are known for their scenic beauty, forested slopes, valleys, and waterfalls.It is composed of sandstone and sedimentary rocks and support diverse flora and fauna.These hills also hold cultural significance as sacred sites integrated into local traditions.Satpura Range lies south of the Mahadeo Hills, spanning Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Chhattisgarh.Named after the Sanskrit word for “seven mountains,” it consists of rolling hills, plateaus, and peaks made of metamorphic and sedimentary rocks.It is home to national parks like Kanha and Satpura.Western Ghats (Sahyadri Parvat) runs along India’s western coast through Maharashtra, Goa, Karnataka, Kerala, and Tamil Nadu.They are composed mainly of basalt rock from ancient volcanic activity.They give rise to rivers like Godavari, Krishna, and Kaveri.The Western Ghats regulate monsoon patterns by intercepting Arabian Sea winds and hold cultural value with ancient temples and sacred sites.Thus, the correct sequence from north to south is: Mahadeo Hills -> Satpura Range ->
