Among the following cities, which one is nearest to the Tropic of Cancer ?

aDelhi
bKolkata
cJodhpur
dNagpur
✓ Correct answer: b) Kolkata
ExplanationIn India, The Tropic of Cancer (23.5° N) passes through 8 Indian states : Gujarat, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Chhat- tisgarh, Jharkhand, West Bengal, Tripura and Mizoram.Location of the given cities in comparison to the Tropic of Cancer is the following : Delhi: 28.7041° N Jodhpur: 26.2389° N Tropic of Cancer: 23.5° N Kolkata: 22.5726° N Nagpur: 21.1458° N Thus, Kolkata is nearest to the Tropic of Cancer.UPSC GS Paper-I PYQs 412 Other cities near to the Tropic of Cancer :Udaipur (Rajasthan): 24.5854° N.Agartala (Tripura): 23.8315° N.Aizawl (Mizoram): 23.7271° N.Ranchi (Jharkhand): 23.3441° N.Bhopal (Madhya Pradesh): 23.2599° N.Gandhinagar (Gujarat): 23.2156° N.Jabalpur (Madhya Pradesh): 23.1815° N.Kolkata (West Bengal): 22.5726° N. The latitude of the Tropic of Cancer is the imaginary circle at approximately 23.5° N.
