If you walk through countryside, you are likely to see some birds stalking alongside the cattle to seize the insects disturbed by their movement through grasses.Which one of the following is/are such bird/birds?1. Painted Stork2. Common Myna3. Black-necked CraneSelect the correct answer using the code given below.

a1 and 2
b2 only
c2 and 3
d3 only
✓ Correct answer: b) 2 only
ExplanationOption 2 is correct: Common mynas are native to South Asia, with their breeding range extending from Afghani- stan through India and Sri Lanka to Bangladesh.They have been introduced to many tropical regions worldwide, except South America.While generally resident in India, occasional east-west movements occur.The Common Myna is an omnivorous, oppor- tunistic feeder, often foraging on insects disturbed by grazing cat- tle.It thrives in urban environments and is known for its strong territorial instincts, often found in open woodlands.Option 1 is incorrect: Painted storks forage in flocks in shallow waters along rivers or lakes.A wading bird that lives in the wet- lands of tropical Asia.They feed on fish, frogs, and insects in shal- low water.It has distinctive pink tertial feathers Option 3 is incorrect: The habitat of Black-necked Crane is in the high altitude wetlands and grasslands of the Tibetan plateau, Sichuan (China), and eastern Ladakh (India).They are not oppor- tunistic feeders.222
