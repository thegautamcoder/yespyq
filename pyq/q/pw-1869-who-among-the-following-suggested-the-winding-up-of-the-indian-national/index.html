Who among the following suggested the winding up of the Indian National Congress after India attained independence?

aC. Rajagopalachari
bAchrya Kripalani
cMahatma Gandhi
dJayaprakash Narain
✓ Correct answer: c) Mahatma Gandhi
ExplanationMahatma Gandhi suggested the winding up of the Indian National Congress after India attained independence.He believed that the Congress had achieved its primary goal of securing independence and should now transform itself into a Lok Sevak Sangh (People's Service Organization) to focus on social and economic reconstruction rather than remaining a political party.His suggestion was not accepted by the Congress leadership.271
