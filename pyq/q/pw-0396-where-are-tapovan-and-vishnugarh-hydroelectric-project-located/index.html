Where are Tapovan and Vishnugarh hydroelectric project located?

aMadhya Pradesh
bUttar Pradesh
cUttarakhand
dRajasthan
✓ Correct answer: c) Uttarakhand
ExplanationThe Tapovan and Vishnugarh hydroelectric projects are lo- cated in Chamoli district of Uttarakhand.These projects utilize the waters of the Dhauliganga River.The Tapovan-Vishnugarh project is a run-of-river scheme, meaning it harnesses the natural flow of the river without creating a large storage reservoir.Developed by NTPC Limited, a major power generation com- pany in India, the Tapovan-Vishnugarh project has a planned installed capacity of 520 MW.This capacity represents a sub- stantial contribution to the power generation capabilities of Ut- tarakhand and the nation.Run-of-river projects like this gen- erally have a smaller environmental footprint compared to large dam projects with vast reservoirs.361
