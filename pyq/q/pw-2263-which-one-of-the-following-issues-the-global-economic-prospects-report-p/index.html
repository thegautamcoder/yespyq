Which one of the following issues the ‘Global Economic Prospects’ report periodically?

aThe Asian Development Bank
bThe European Bank for Reconstruction and Development
cThe US Federal Reserve Bank
dThe World Bank UPSC GS Paper-I PYQs 658
✓ Correct answer: d) The World Bank UPSC GS Paper-I PYQs 658
ExplanationThe World Bank is the publisher of the Global Eco- nomic Prospects report, a flagship publication that provides comprehensive analyses of the global economic landscape.This report is released twice a year, typically in January and June, and offers in-depth assessments of current economic conditions, emerging trends, and potential risks to the global economy.It serves as a key resource for policymakers.It focuses on the challenges faced by emerging markets and developing economies (EMDEs).The report highlights key is- sues such as trade restrictions, inflation, debt, and geopolit- ical tensions, while offering policy recommendations to foster sustainable growth.For 2025-26, global growth is projected to remain at 2.7%, with EMDEs contributing significantly to global GDP.The GEP emphasizes the need for investment, fiscal reforms, and international cooperation to address climate change, trade governance, and economic disparities.77
