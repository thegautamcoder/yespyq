What is the correct chronological order in which the following appeared in India?1. Gold Coin2. Punch marked Silver Coin3. Iron Plough4. Urban CultureSelect the correct answer using the codes given below:

a3, 4, 1, 2
b3, 4, 2, 1
c4, 3, 1, 2
d4, 3, 2, 1
✓ Correct answer: d) 4, 3, 2, 1
ExplanationThe correct order of development (from oldest to newest): Urban Culture arose with the Indus Valley Civilization (~2600 BCE), marking the emergence of planned urban centers.(4) Iron Plough usage began around the 1st millennium BCE, enhancing agricultural output.(3) Punch-marked Silver Coins started circulating by the 6th century BCE as early currency.(2) Gold Coins were introduced during the Kushan period, establishing high-value currency standards.(1) 77
