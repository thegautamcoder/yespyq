If the National Water Mission is properly and completely implemented, how will it impact the country?1. Part of the water needs of urban areas will be met through recycling of waste-water.2. The water requirements of coastal cities with inadequate alternative sources of water will be met by adopting appropriate technologies that allow for the use of ocean water.3. All the rivers of Himalayan origin will be linked to the rivers of peninsular India.4. The expenses incurred by farmers for digging bore-wells and for installing motors and pump-sets to draw ground-water will be completely reimbursed by the Government.Select the correct answer using the codes given below:

a1 only
b1 and 2 only
c3 and 4 only
d1, 2, 3 and 4
✓ Correct answer: b) 1 and 2 only
