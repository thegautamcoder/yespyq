Which of the following are among the million-plus cities in India on the basis of data of the Census, 2001 ?1. Ludhiana2. Kochi3. Surat4. NagpurSelect the correct answer using the code given below:Codes :

a1, 2 and 3 only
b2, 3 and 4 only
c1 and 4 only
d1, 2, 3 and 4
✓ Correct answer: d) 1, 2, 3 and 4
ExplanationThe term “million-plus cities” refers to urban agglomerations with a population exceeding one million.Based on the Census of India data below, the populations of the selected cities in 2001 and 2011 are as follows : UPSC GS Paper-I PYQs 376 City Population in 2001 Population in 2011 Ludhiana 1,398,467 1,618,879 Kochi Urban Agglomeration 1,355,406 2,117,990 Surat 2,433,835 4,467,797 Nagpur 2,052,066 2,405,421 Hence, cities—Ludhiana, Surat, Kochi and Nagpur—were clas- sified as million-plus cities in India based on the 2001 census data.151
