Match List I (Beaches in India) with List II (States) and select the correct answer using the codes given below : List-I List-IIA. Gopinath Beach1. Andhra PradeshB. Lawsons Bay Beach2. KeralaC. Devbagh Beach3. GujaratD. Sinquerim Beach4. Goa5. KarnatakaCodes :

aA-5; B-4; C-2; D-1
bA-3; B-1; C-5; D-4
cA-5; B-1; C-2; D-4
dA-3; B-4; C-5; D-1
✓ Correct answer: b) A-3; B-1; C-5; D-4
ExplanationA is correctly matched with 3 : Gopinath Beach is located near the town of Bhavnagar in Gujarat.It is known for its rocky shores and breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea.The beach is named after the Gopinath Mahadev Temple located nearby.B is correctly matched with 1: Lawson’s Bay Beach is situat- ed in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.This scenic and serene beach is an extension of Ramakrishna Beach and is popular for swimming and sunbathing.It is a clean and less crowded des- tination compared to other beaches in Vizag.C is correctly matched with 5: Devbagh Beach is located near Karwar in Karnataka.It’s known for its pristine sands, clear wa- ters, and the serene environment.The area surrounding Devbagh is also known for its natural beauty, including the backwaters and the nearby islands.D is correctly matched with 4: Sinquerim Beach is a popular beach in Goa located near the historic Aguada Fort.It is well known for water sports like jet skiing, windsurfing, and ba- nana boat rides.The beach is a favorite among tourists for its golden sands and stunning sunsets.385
