🏛️ History & Art-CultureFirst Phase of Revolutionary Activities (1907-1917)

Consider the following freedom fighters: (UPSC Prelims, 2022)1. Barindra Kumar Ghosh2. Jogesh Chandra Chatterjee3. Rash Behari Bose Who of the above personalities was/were actively associated with the Ghadar Party?

a1 and 2
b2 only
c1 and 3
d3 only
✓ Correct answer: d) 3 only
ExplanationAns: D Exp:Statement 1 is incorrect: Barindra Kumar Ghosh was associated with a revolutionary group called Anushilan Samiti.In the Alipore Bomb Case, he and Ulaskar Dutta were sentenced to death, and due to the intervention of C.R. Das, the sentence was commuted to life imprisonment and deported to a Cellular Jail, in Andaman.Statement 2 is incorrect: Jogesh Chatterjee served in the Hindustan Republican Association/Army (HRA), later joined the Revolutionary Socialist Party (RSP) after independence, and became a member of the Rajya Sabha from the Congress Party in 1956. Statement 3 is correct: The prominent leaders associated with the Ghadar Party were Lala Hardayal, Bhagwan Singh, Ramchandra, Kartar Singh Saraba, Barkatullah, Bhai Parmanand, Raghubar Dayal Gupta, Rash Behari Bose, and Sachin Sanyal were involved in the movement.
