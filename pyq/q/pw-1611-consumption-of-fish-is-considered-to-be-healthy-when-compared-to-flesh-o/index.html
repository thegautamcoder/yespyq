Consumption of fish is considered to be healthy when compared to flesh of other animals because fish contains:

apolyunsaturated fatty acids
bsaturated fatty acids
cessential vitamins
dmore carbohydrates and proteins
✓ Correct answer: a) polyunsaturated fatty acids
ExplanationFish, especially oily fish like salmon, mackerel, and sardines are rich in polyunsaturated fatty acids (PUFAs) such as omega-3 fatty acids (e.g., EPA and DHA).These fatty acids are essential for various health benefits such as: Reducing the risk of cardiovascular diseases.Improving brain function and development.Reducing inflammation.498
