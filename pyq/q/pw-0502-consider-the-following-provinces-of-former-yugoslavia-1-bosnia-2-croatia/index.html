Consider the following provinces of former Yugoslavia :1. Bosnia2. Croatia3. Slovenia4. Yugoslavia The correct sequence of these provinces from the east to west is :

a4, 1, 3, 2
b4, 1, 2, 3
c1, 4, 3, 2
d1, 4, 2, 3
✓ Correct answer: b) 4, 1, 2, 3
ExplanationYugoslavia : The Federal Republic of Yugoslavia, as it existed circa 2000, was a federation comprising Serbia and Montenegro.Geographically, it occupied the easternmost por- tion of the area in question, bordering the other listed entities.It is important to note that this federation was in the process of dissolution during this period i.e 2000. Bosnia : Bosnia and Herzegovina, often referred to simply as Bosnia, is located west of the former Yugoslavia.It is a country situated in Southeastern Europe, occupying a significant portion of the Balkan Peninsula.Its geography is characterized by moun- tainous terrain and diverse cultural influences.Croatia : The Republic of Croatia is situated further west than Bosnia and Herzegovina.Bordering the Adriatic Sea, it is also located in Southeastern Europe.Croatia is known for its exten- sive coastline, numerous islands, and historic cities, reflecting its complex history.Geography 463 Slovenia : The Republic of Slovenia is the westernmost of the four entities listed.Located in Central Europe, it is known for its mountainous landscape
