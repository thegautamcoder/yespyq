The striking feature of the Jama Masjid in Kashmir completed by Zain-ul-Abidin include(s):1. Turret2. Similarity with Buddhist pagodas3. Persian styleWhich of the above statements is/are correct?

a1 alone
b1, 2 and 3
c2 and 3
d1 and 3
✓ Correct answer: b) 1, 2 and 3
ExplanationThe Jama Masjid in Kashmir, completed under the patronage of Zain-ul-Abidin (1420 – 1470 AD), is renowned for its unique blend of architectural features, including Persian influences, turrets, and a resemblance to Buddhist pagodas.This architectural synthesis reflects Kashmir’s historical interaction with Persian culture and Buddhist heritage.38
