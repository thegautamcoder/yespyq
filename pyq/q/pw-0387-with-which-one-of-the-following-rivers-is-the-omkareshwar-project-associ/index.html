With which one of the following rivers is the Omkareshwar Project associated?

aChambal
bNarmada
cTapi
dBhima
✓ Correct answer: b) Narmada
ExplanationThe Omkareshwar Project, a multi-purpose dam project, is located in the Khandwa district of Madhya Pradesh, India.This project utilizes the Narmada River, a ma- jor river system in central India, to achieve several key objectives such as:A primary function is the generation of hydroelectric power, with a station capacity of 520 MW, contributing substantially to the regional energy supply.The project is intended to provide irrigation to extensive agricultural lands, thereby increasing agricultural productivity and supporting local economies.The Omkareshwar Project also plays a role in supplying water for domestic and industrial use, ensuring access to this vital resource.The dam itself is situated on the Narmada River, proximate to the town of Omkareshwar, a significant Hindu pilgrimage site.The town’s religious importance derives from the presence of the Omkareshwar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva.Geography 425 351
