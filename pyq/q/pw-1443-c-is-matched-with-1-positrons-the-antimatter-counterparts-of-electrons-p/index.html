Assertion (A): Large cold storage plants use ammonia as refrigerant while domestic refrigerators use chlorofluoro- carbons. Reason (R): Ammonia can be liquefied at ambient temperature and low pressure.

aBoth A and R are true and R is the correct explanation of A
bBoth A and R are true but R is not a correct explanation of A
cA is true but R is false
dA is false but R is true
✓ Correct answer: c) A is true but R is false
ExplanationAssertion (A) is true: Large cold storage plants often use ammonia as a refrigerant because it has high efficiency in transferring heat.Ammonia is widely used in industrial refrigeration due to its thermodynamic properties and relatively low cost.Domestic refrigerators, on the other hand, typically use chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs) or their replacements, like HFCs, due to their non-toxic and non-flammable nature.Reason (R) is false: Ammonia cannot be liquefied at ambient temperature and low pressure.It requires a higher pressure or lower temperature to be liquefied.Ammonia is typically stored and transported as a liquid under pressure or at low temperatures.Therefore, while the assertion is correct, the reason is false.308
