The efforts to detect the existence of the Higgs boson particle have become frequent news in the recent past. What is/are the importance/importances of discovering this particle?1. It will enable us to understand why elementary particles have mass.2. It will enable us in the near future to develop the technology of transferring matter from one point to another without traversing the physical space between them.3. It will enable us to create better fuels for nuclear missions.Select the correct answer using the codes given below:

a1 only
b2 and 3 only
c1 and 3 only
d1, 2 and 3 UPSC GS Paper-I PYQs 560
✓ Correct answer: a) 1 only
ExplanationStatement 1 is correct: The Also known as " God Particle" is associated with the Higgs field, which permeates the universe.Elementary particles acquire mass through their interaction with this field.Without the Higgs mechanism, particles would remain massless, preventing the formation of atoms and, consequently, the existence of matter as we know it.This discovery confirms the Standard Model’s explanation of mass acquisition.Statement 2 is incorrect: The Part of Invisible energy field ,if Higgs field doesn't ok provide mass to particles then there will be no gravity and no life. is not a key item that will open the way for teleportation but it will certainly help to understand mass , time and travel in time and space.Statement 3 is incorrect: In the near or far future, the Higgs Boson may help us to create an unbelievable energy source which can pave the way for better fuels for our Nuclear missions but at present, direct connection between detection of Higgs Bosons and creation of better fuels for nuclear missions is not established.118
