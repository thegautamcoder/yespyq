Create meaningful short stories and songs3. Disease diagnosis4. Text-to-Speech Conversion5. Wireless transmission of electrical energy Science and Technology 547Select the correct answer using the code given below:

a1, 2, 3 and 5 only
b1, 3 and 4 only
c2, 4 and 5 only
d1, 2, 3, 4 and 5
