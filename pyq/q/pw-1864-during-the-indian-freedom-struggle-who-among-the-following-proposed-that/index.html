During the Indian freedom struggle, who among the following proposed that Swaraj should be defined as complete independence free from all foreign control?

aMazharul Haque
bMaulana Hasrat Mohani
cHakim Ajmal Khan
dAbul Kalam Azad
✓ Correct answer: b) Maulana Hasrat Mohani
ExplanationMaulana Hasrat Mohani, a prominent figure in the Indian freedom movement, was a strong advocate for complete independence from British rule.He was one of the key leaders who pushed for the idea that Swaraj, or self-rule, should be interpreted as absolute freedom, devoid of any foreign influence or control.His stance was a significant departure from the moderate demands for self-governance and paved the way for more radical calls for full sovereignty during the independence struggle.Hasrat Mohani started bringing out a magazine in the name of ‘Urdu-e-Mualla’ in 1903. 266
