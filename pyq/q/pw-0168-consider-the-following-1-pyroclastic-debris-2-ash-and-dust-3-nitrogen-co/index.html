Consider the following :1. Pyroclastic debris2. Ash and dust3. Nitrogen compounds4. Sulphur compoundsHow many of the above are products of volcanic eruptions ?

aOnly one
bOnly two
cOnly three
dAll four
✓ Correct answer: d) All four
ExplanationA volcano is an opening or rupture in the Earth’s crust through which molten rock (magma), gases, and ash es- cape from beneath the surface.When magma reaches the surface, it is called lava.Volcanic eruptions can shape mountains and is- lands, affecting both the environment and climate.Example : Mount Etna(Italy), Kilauea (USA) The materials that reach the Earth’s surface in volcanoes in- clude :lava flowspyroclastic debrisvolcanic bombsash and dustgases such as nitrogen compoundssulphur compoundsminor amounts of chlorine, hydrogen and argon etc. SUPER HINT You can try, The "NOT" approach for all statements, this tests the improbability of negating a statement—if denying its impact seems highly unlikely, the statement is plausibly true.For example: Can I deny Pyroclastic flow, or gases or ash are not products of Volcanoes?Likely No!, Hence all are likely true.95
