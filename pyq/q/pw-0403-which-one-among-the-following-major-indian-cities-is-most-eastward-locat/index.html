Which one among the following major Indian cities is most eastward located?

aHyderabad
bBhopal
cLucknow
dBengaluru (Bangalore)
✓ Correct answer: c) Lucknow
ExplanationThe approximate location coordinates for each city:Hyderabad: 17.4065° N, 78.4772° EBhopal: 23.2599° N, 77.4126° ELucknow: 26.8467° N, 80.9462° EBengaluru (Bangalore): 12.9716° N, 77.5946° E As Lucknow has the highest longitude value, making it the most eastwardly located city among the four.368
