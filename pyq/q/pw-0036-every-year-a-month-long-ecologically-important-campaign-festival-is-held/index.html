Every year, a month-long ecologically important campaign/festival is held during which certain communities/tribes plant saplings of fruit-bearing trees. Which of the following are such communities/ tribes?

aBhutia and Lepcha
bGond and Korku
cIrula and Toda
dSahariya and Agariya
✓ Correct answer: b) Gond and Korku
ExplanationEvery monsoon the Gond and Korku tribes of Betul and Harda districts of Madhya Pradesh celebrate a month- long festival known as Hari Jiroti.This festival is dedicated to promoting greenery and environmental conservation.During Hari Jiroti community members engage in the plantation of saplings, especially those of fruit-bearing trees such as mango, guava, and jamun.This practice not only enhances the local ecosystem but also ensures a sustainable source of food and income for future generations.41
