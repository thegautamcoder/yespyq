Which one of the following National Parks has a climate that varies from tropical to subtropical, temperate and arctic?

aKhangchendzonga National Park
bNanda Devi National Park
cNeora Valley National Park
dNamdapha National Park
✓ Correct answer: d) Namdapha National Park
ExplanationThe Namdapha National Park is located at the inter- national border between India and Myanmar (Burma) in Changlang District, Arunachal Pradesh in northeast India.It is known for its unique climate that ranges from tropical to subtropical, temperate, and arctic due to its vast altitude variation.Spanning from 200 meters to 4,500 meters above sea level, the park encompasses a wide range of ecosystems.The lower altitudes experience tropical and subtropical climates with rich biodiversity, including dense forests and diverse wildlife.As you move higher, the climate shifts to temperate, and eventually, at the highest elevations, it reaches an arctic-like climate, hosting snow-covered peaks.This diversity in climate allows the park to support an incredible variety of flora and fauna, including spe- cies like the snow leopard, clouded leopard, and tiger.Environment and Ecology 517 186
