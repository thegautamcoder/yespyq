Holiday resortC. Nhava Sheva3. Petrochemical complexD. Ratnagiri4. PortCodes :

aA-1; B-3; C-2; D-4
bA-2; B-3; C-4; D-1
cA-3; B-4; C-2; D-1
dA-2; B-1; C-4; D-3 Geography 383
✓ Correct answer: d) A-2; B-1; C-4; D-3 Geography 383
