Consider the following functionaries:1. Cabinet Secretary2. Chief Election Commissioner3. Union Cabinet Minister4. Chief Justice of India Their correct sequence in the Order of Precedence is:

a3, 4, 2, 1
b4, 3, 1, 2
c4, 3, 2, 1
d3, 4, 1, 2 Polity 235
✓ Correct answer: c) 4, 3, 2, 1
ExplanationThe Order of Precedence is a protocol list maintained by the Government of India that determines the ceremonial hierarchy for officials.The Chief Justice of India (CJI) ranks the highest among the given functionaries, occupying the 6th position in the Order of Precedence.Next is the Union Cabinet Minister, who ranks 7th, reflecting their high status as senior members of the executive.The Chief Election Commissioner comes next in rank, placed lower than a Union Cabinet Minister in ceremonial hierarchy.Finally, the Cabinet Secretary, though the highest-ranking civil servant in India, ranks lower than the other three functionaries, holding a position below the Chief Election Commissioner.Below is the Order of Precedence as per the latest guidelines:1. President of India2. Vice President of India3. Prime Minister of India4. Governors of States (within their respective states)5. Former Presidents Chief Justice of India Speaker of the Lok Sabha6.
