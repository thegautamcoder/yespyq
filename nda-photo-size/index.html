Frequently asked questions

What is the NDA photo size for the online application?
For most cycles the UPSC NDA form wants a passport-style colour photo at 400×400 pixels, 20–100 KB in JPG, set against a plain white background.
What is the NDA signature size?
The signature runs around 400×200 pixels and 10–50 KB in JPG. Sign it clearly with black ink on white paper.
Can girls apply for NDA and submit a photo?
Absolutely. Women have been able to sit NDA since 2022, and they follow exactly the same photo rules as everyone else.
What kind of photo should I use for NDA?
Go for a formal head-on colour shot in a collared shirt or formal wear, on a plain white background, looking straight into the lens — no cap, sunglasses or jewellery.
Does NDA require a recent photo?
It does. The photo should be from the last six months, and you should reuse the same one right through the NDA process — SSB, medical and the rest.
What is the NDA age limit?
The age band is a tight 16.5 to 19.5 years, measured on the first day of the month the course begins, and no category gets any relaxation. Our NDA age calculator can check yours.
