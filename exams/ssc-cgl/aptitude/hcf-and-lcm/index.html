Q1
In finding the HCF of two numbers by division method, the last divisor is 17 and the quotients are 1,11 and 2 , respectively. What is sum of the two numbers?
a867
b816
c901
d833
✓ Correct answer: b) 816
ExplanationAs we know, Divided = Divisor × quotient + remainder Divided = 17 × 2 + 0 Divided = 34 Again, Divided = Divisor × quotient + remainder Divided = 34 × 11 + 17 Divided = 374 + 17 = 391 Again, Divided = Divisor × quotient + remainder Divided = 391 × 1 + 34 Divided = 391 + 34 = 425 The two numbers are 425 and 391 Sum of two numbers = 425 + 391 = 816 Hence, Option B is the correct answer.
Q2
LCM and HCF of two numbers are 90 and 15 , respectively. If the sum of the two numbers is 75 , then find the greater number.
a45
b90
c75
d60
✓ Correct answer: a) 45
ExplanationLet the numbers are 15a and 15b respectively.Also, let the greater number be 15a.15ab = 90 ⇒ ab = 6…..(1) 15a + 15b = 75 ⇒ a + b = 5…..(2) From (1) and (2) we get: a = 3, b = 2 So, the greater number = 15 × 3 = 45 Hence, the correct answer is (A).

Q8
The largest three-digit number that gives the same remainder 2 when divided by 3, 5 and 9 is ________.
a999
b984
c998
d992
✓ Correct answer: d) 992
ExplanationTo find the largest three-digit number that gives the same remainder of 2 when divided by 3, 5, and 9, we first need to find the least common multiple (LCM) of these three divisors.LCM(3, 5, 9) =45 Now we need to find the largest three-digit multiple of 45 and add the remainder (2) to it.The largest three-digit multiple of 45 is 990 ( ) Now, add the remainder 2 to this multiple: 990+2=992 So, the largest three-digit number that gives the same remainder 2 when divided by 3, 5, and 9 is =992 Hence, Option D is the correct answer.
