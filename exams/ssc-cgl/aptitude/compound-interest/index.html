Q10
A sum of Rs.15,000 is lent at 16 % p.a. compound interest. What is the difference between the compound interest for the second year and the third year?
a₹ 544
b₹ 445.44
c₹ 454.88
d₹ 548
✓ Correct answer: b) ₹ 445.44
ExplanationP = Rs. 15,000, r = 16%, T = 2 years Compound interest only for 2nd year ⇒ 15000 × (16/100) = 2400 ⇒ 2400 × (16/100) = 384 Compound interest only for 2nd year = 2400 + 384 = Rs. 2784 Compound interest only for 3rd year ⇒ 15000 × (16/100) = 2400 × 1 = 2400 ⇒ 2400 × (16/100) = 384 × 2 = 768 ⇒ 384 × (16/100) = 61.44 Compound interest of 3 years = 2400 + 768 + 61.44 = 3,229.44 ∴ Required difference = 3,229.44 – 2,784 = Rs. 445.44 Hence, Option B is the correct answer.
