Q6
The difference between the cubes of two given natural numbers is 6272 , while the positive difference between the two given numbers is 8 . What is the product of the two given numbers?
a160
b240
c200
d320
✓ Correct answer: b) 240
ExplanationLet's denote the two natural numbers as a and b, where a>b.The difference between the cubes of the two numbers is 6272: a³ - b³ = 6272 (i) The positive difference between the two numbers is 8: a - b = 8 (ii) a = b + 8 Substitute this value of a into the first equation: (b+8)³ - b³ = 6272 b³ + 24b² + 192b + 512 - b³ = 6272 24b² + 192b + 512 = 6272 24b² + 192b = 5760 b² + 8b = 240 b² + 8b + 16 = 240 + 16 (b + 4)² = 256 b + 4 = 16 b = 12 b = -20 (which is not possible as we are dealing with natural numbers) Since we are looking for positive difference, we consider b = 12, which leads to ab = 12 x 20 = 240 So, the product of the two given natural numbers is 240. Hence, option B is correct
