Q1
Satya's birthday falls on 15th August and Meena's birthday falls on 25th June. If Meena's birthday was on Wednesday, what was the day on Sathya's birthday in the same year?
aFriday
bMonday
cTuesday
dSaturday
✓ Correct answer: a) Friday
ExplanationGiven: Meena’s birthday: 25 June (Wednesday) Satya’s birthday: 15 August (same year) Step 1: Count days between 25 June and 15 August Remaining days in June = 30−25=5 days Days in July = 31 days Days in August up to 15th = 15 days Total days = 5+31+15=51 days Step 2: Find day shift 51÷7=7 weeks + 2 days So the day advances by 2 days Step 3: Move 2 days ahead from Wednesday Wednesday → Thursday → Friday Hence, friday is the correct answer.
Q2
Divit's birthday is on Friday 30th June. On what day of the week will be Samar's Birthday in the same year if Samar was born on 15th November?
aTuesday
bFriday
cWednesday
dSaturday
✓ Correct answer: c) Wednesday
ExplanationDays left in June = 30 – 30 = 0 Days in November to count = 15 Thus, total number of days from Divit's birthday to Samar’s birthday = 31 + 31 + 30 + 31 + 15 = 138 Thus, number of odd days = 138 | 7 = 5 ...........(applying Modulo function which gives the remainder of the division.This is used here to separate the number of odd days from the number of weeks.) As Divit's birthday is on Friday, Samar’s birthday will on Friday + 5 days = Wednesday.Hence, Wednesday is the correct answer.

✓ Correct answer: d) Thursday

aTuesday
bWednesday
cMonday
dThursday
✓ Correct answer: b) Wednesday
ExplanationHere, in this question we have to find what day of the week was 31 January 2007. To find a particular day of the year, we need to follow some steps to- Step 1: No. of odd days No. of odd days Leap year Normal year 366 ÷ 7 = 52 weeks + 2 odd days 365 ÷ 7 = 52 weeks + 1 odd day Step 2: To find leap year (1-99) years, divide the number of years by 4 and quotient will be the number of leap year Step 2: Code for week days No. of odd days Day 0 Sunday 1 Monday 2 Tuesday 3 Wednesday 4 Thursday 5 Friday 6 Saturday Now, upto year 2006 ➡ 2000 + 6 years No. of odd days in year 2000 is 0 No. of odd days in year 6 years (1 leap yr and 5 normal yr) is 2+5 = 7 Now for January (31 days) month of year 2007, no. of odd days is 3 Therefore total odd days ➡ 0+7+3= 10 days (1 week + 3 days).And, we know that 3 is the code for “Wednesday” Hence, Wednesday is the correct answer.

Q25
Which of the following calendars will be the same as the calendar for the year 2009 ?
a2025
b2015
c2036
d2042
✓ Correct answer: b) 2015
ExplanationThe calendars for the years 2009 and 2015 are the same because both years are ordinary years.An ordinary year has 365 days and 1 odd day.The odd day is Sunday in 2009 and 2015. The calendars for the years 2025, 2036, and 2042 are not the same as the calendar for the year 2009 because they are leap years.A leap year has 366 days and 2 odd days.The first odd day is Saturday in 2025, Sunday in 2036, and Monday in 2042. The second odd day is Monday in 2025 and 2036, and Tuesday in 2042. Therefore, the correct answer is B. 2015.

dTuesday
✓ Correct answer: c) Saturday
ExplanationIn this question, we have to find If exactly 1 year and 2 days later it is Tuesday, then what day is it today.It is not a leap year.1 year = 365 days (non leap year) So the total number of days = 365 + 2 = 367 days So, 367 is divided by 7 Then the remainder is 3 Therefore 1 year and 2 days later is Tuesday So, today's day ⇒ Tuesday - 3 = Saturday Hence, "Saturday" is the correct answer.
