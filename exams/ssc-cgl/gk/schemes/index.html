Schemes

4 solved SSC CGL General Knowledge previous year questions on Schemes, each with the correct answer and a full explanation.

Q1
In February 2022, Maharashtra government announced ‘Hope Express’ initiative to ______.
aprevent cancer
bimprove drainage system
cmanage industrial waste
dimprove air quality
✓ Correct answer: a) prevent cancer
ExplanationIn February 2022, the Maharashtra government launched the ‘Hope Express’ initiative as a part of its public health outreach programs.The main aim of this initiative is to prevent cancer by creating awareness, early detection, and facilitating screening for citizens across the state.The program uses specialized trains and mobile units to reach remote and rural areas where medical facilities may be limited.It focuses on educating people about cancer risks, symptoms, and treatment options, helping in early diagnosis and reducing mortality rates.Hence, the correct answer is “prevent cancer”.
Q2
The West Bengal government has launched 'Paray Shikshalaya’ programme. This initiative supports _______.
aopen air classroom
bfinancial assistance for girl child
cblackboard distribution in every school
dhigher education for class 10th students
✓ Correct answer: a) open air classroom
ExplanationOn February 7, 2022, the government of West Bengal introduced a scheme called "Paray Shikshalaya," which is an open-air school in the neighbourhood that is designed for pupils in classes 1 through7. The purpose of this effort is to persuade students who left their education unfinished because to the COVID-19 outbreak to return to school and finish what they started.Hence, the correct answer is "A) open air classroom."
Q3
Which of the following launched NEAT 3.0 to provide best developed ed-tech solutions, in January 2022?
aMinistry of Law and Justice
bMinistry of Electronics and Information Technology
cMinistry of Social Justice and Empowerment
dMinistry of Education
✓ Correct answer: d) Ministry of Education
ExplanationThe Ministry of Education launched NEAT 3.0 (National Educational Alliance for Technology) in January 2022. NEAT 3.0 is aimed at providing high- quality, technology-driven educational solutions to enhance the learning experience for students across India.This initiative focuses on promoting the best ed-tech solutions developed by various startups and educational technology providers.By integrating technology into education, NEAT 3.0 aims to bridge the gap between students and quality educational resources, making learning more accessible and effective.The initiative is part of the government's broader efforts to leverage technology in education and support the New Education Policy (NEP) goals.Hence, the correct answer is "Ministry of Education."
Q4
In August 2021, the Ministry of Labour and Employment launched the ________ portal for creating a National Database of Unorganized Workers (UWs).
aeUmang
beRozgaar
ceGyan
deShram
✓ Correct answer: d) eShram
ExplanationThe Ministry of Labour and Employment launched the eShram portal in August 2021 to create a comprehensive National Database of Unorganized Workers (UWs).This portal aims to register and provide social security benefits to workers in the unorganized sector, who often lack formal employment records.The database helps the government identify and deliver welfare schemes directly to unorganized workers.By registering on eShram, workers receive a unique identification number, ensuring streamlined access to benefits.The initiative is a part of the government’s efforts to improve the welfare of unorganized workers and provide better employment and social security benefits.Hence, the correct answer is "eShram."
