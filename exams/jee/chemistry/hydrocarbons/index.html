Q1
Which of the following is a free radical substitution reaction? [Re-NEET 2020]
aAcetylene with
bMethane with
cPropene with
dBenzene with
✓ Correct answer: b) Methane with
ExplanationMethane + Br₂ (hv) is the classic example of free radical substitution, where light initiates the radical chain process.(a) is an addition to an alkyne, (c) is radical addition across a double bond, and (d) is electrophilic aromatic substitution.
