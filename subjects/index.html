Why practise UPSC PYQs subject-wise?

The UPSC Prelims rewards pattern recognition. When you solve previous year questions grouped by subject, the Commission's recurring themes — the same constitutional ideas, the same conservation-status traps, the same economic definitions — become impossible to miss. Each subject page below distils the high-yield topics and links straight into focused practice.
