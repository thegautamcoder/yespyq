Frequently asked questions

What is the RRB Group D photo size?
A recent colour photo at 320×240 pixels, 50–100 KB, in JPG, on a plain white or light background, with the face taking up at least half the frame.
What is the RRB Group D signature size?
140×60 pixels, 30–50 KB, in JPG. It must be your normal running (cursive) signature in black ink — block or capital letters are rejected.
Are RRB Group D and RRB NTPC photo specs the same?
They're close but not identical across every CEN notification. Group D commonly asks for a larger 50–100 KB photo versus NTPC's lighter cap, so resize separately for each and always confirm against the live notification.
Why does RRB reject signature uploads?
The most common reasons are signing in capital/block letters instead of your normal running signature, using pencil or a colour other than black ink, or missing the 140×60 px / 30–50 KB window.
How old can the RRB Group D photo be?
Keep it recent — most notifications ask for a photo taken within the last few months, not an old or scanned passport photo.
